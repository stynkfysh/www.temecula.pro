Appraisal Services & Fees

Transparent pricing for estate, divorce, litigation, pre-purchase, and specialized appraisals throughout Temecula and Riverside County.

Appraisal Pricing

Competitive, transparent rates. All prices include a written report suitable for courts, attorneys, and the IRS. Fees listed are starting prices — complex, high-liability, large-acreage, or rush properties may be higher, and your exact fee is quoted before you commit.

What You Receive

A professional written report suitable for courts, attorneys, the IRS, lenders, and financial institutions — delivered digitally, with comprehensive market analysis, property description, and a defensible value conclusion.

Turnaround Time

Most residential reports are completed within several business days. I work efficiently to meet court deadlines and time-sensitive transactions — contact me to discuss your timeline.

Payment Options

Payment by credit card, check, or electronic transfer, due upon completion. Contact me to discuss invoice terms for large assignments or multi-property estate work.
