Bankruptcy Appraisals in Temecula & Riverside County

A bankruptcy appraisal provides the credible, independent opinion of value that trustees, attorneys, and the bankruptcy court require to determine the equity in real property. Whether you are filing Chapter 7 or Chapter 13, an accurate appraisal protects your interests and supports your petition.

Why an Appraisal Matters in Bankruptcy

The value of your home directly affects how much equity is exposed, whether liens can be stripped, and how the trustee treats the asset. An online estimate is not sufficient — a certified appraisal carries the weight needed in court.

Accepted by Trustees and Courts

My reports comply with USPAP and are written to be accepted by bankruptcy trustees and the court. I can provide current market value or a retrospective value tied to your filing date.

Fast Turnaround

Bankruptcy filings run on deadlines. I work efficiently to deliver a complete report in time for your petition or hearing.

Pricing

Bankruptcy appraisals start at $299 for standard residential properties. Contact me to confirm scope and turnaround for your filing. See the full fee schedule for all report types.
